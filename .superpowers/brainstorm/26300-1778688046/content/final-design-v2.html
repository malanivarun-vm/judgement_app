One structural change from earlier mockup: The "Selected bid" panel (large 42px gold number) has been removed. The gold chip in the swipe row makes it obvious which number is selected — keeping both would make the modal too tall on smaller phones. The haptic feedback on selection still fires to confirm the choice. Everything else is identical to the approved design.
