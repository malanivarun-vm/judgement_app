A

Bottom Sheet (Recommended)

Sheet slides up from bottom, hand stays visible above it. Light backdrop preserves game context. Smallest code change — just swap Modal for a bottom sheet.

B

Cards Inside the Modal

Modal stays center-screen but shows a read-only card strip at the top. No layout change. Works well if the table context doesn't matter much during bidding.

C

Inline / No Modal

Remove the modal entirely. Bid selector lives inline at the bottom, hand above it. Most elegant long-term, but requires a more significant layout refactor of game.tsx.
