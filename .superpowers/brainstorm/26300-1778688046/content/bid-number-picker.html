1

Swipe row

Numbers in a flat scrollable row. Swipe left to see higher numbers. Familiar, simple to build. Restricted bid visible as red chip.

2

– / + Stepper (Recommended)

Tap minus or plus to change the number. No overflow ever. Restricted bid is auto-skipped. Large thumb targets. Works identically for 5-card and 17-card rounds.

3

Drum roll picker

Drag vertically like an iOS wheel. Most native-feeling on mobile. Restricted bid is skipped mid-scroll. Most complex to implement.
