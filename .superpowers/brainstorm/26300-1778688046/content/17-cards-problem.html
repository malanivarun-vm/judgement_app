17 Cards — How Does the Hand Render?

3 players → floor(52/3) = 17 cards in round 1. The hand dock needs to handle this gracefully across all three approaches.

The problem: With 17 cards, the horizontal scroll hand dock shows ~5–6 cards at a time. To bid intelligently, you need to see all 17. Scrolling defeats the purpose — you'd need to scrub back and forth just to assess your hand.
